My name is Brandon Moore

Who am I?

I am a third year Computer Science and Engineering student at The Ohio State University. At OSU I was introduced to Linux, and have been transfixed ever since. I currently run Arch Linux on my two machines, and I am the President of the Open Source club at OSU. I enjoy the learning experience offerred not just in the classroom, but in clubs, hackathons, and from my peers as well. I am most happy with a terminal open, typing away on a project in Vim.
